# Acme Store — Customer Support FAQ

## Returns & Refunds

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Bring your receipt or order confirmation email to any Acme Store location, or ship the item back using the prepaid return label from your account. Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days to your original payment method.

## Shipping

Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days and is free on orders over $50. Express shipping (2 business days) is available for $9.99. Same-day delivery is available in selected cities — check eligibility at checkout. Once your order ships you will receive a tracking email.

## Order Tracking

Log in to your account at acme.example and go to Orders. Click your order number to see real-time tracking. You can also track using the link in your shipping confirmation email without logging in.

## Password Reset

Go to acme.example/login and click "Forgot password". Enter your registered email address and we will send a reset link within 5 minutes. The link expires in 24 hours. Check your spam folder if you do not receive it.

## Billing & Payment

Log in and go to Account > Payment Methods. You can add, remove, or set a default card. We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. Changes take effect immediately for your next purchase.
