# Acme Store — Customer Support FAQ

## Returns & Refunds

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging with all tags attached. To start a return, log in to your account at acme.example, go to Orders, select the item, and click "Return Item" — you will receive a prepaid return label by email within 15 minutes. You can also bring the item and your receipt or order confirmation email to any Acme Store location for an in-store return.

Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days to your original payment method once we receive and inspect the item. If you paid by PayPal, the refund appears in your PayPal balance. If your item arrived damaged or defective, we will send a replacement at no cost — see the Damaged or Defective Items section below.

Items marked as Final Sale, perishables, digital downloads, and customised products cannot be returned unless they are defective.

## Shipping

Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days and is free on orders over $50. Orders below $50 are charged a flat $4.99 shipping fee. Express shipping (1–2 business days) is available for $9.99. Overnight delivery is available for $19.99 on orders placed before 1 pm local time on business days. Same-day delivery is available in selected cities — check eligibility by entering your postcode at checkout.

Once your order ships you will receive a tracking email with a link to follow your parcel in real time. Estimated delivery dates are shown at checkout and in your order confirmation.

Shipping is available to all 50 US states, US territories, and APO/FPO addresses. For international shipping see the International Orders section below.

## Order Tracking

Log in to your account at acme.example and go to Orders. Click your order number to see the current status and real-time tracking map. You can also track without logging in using the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email.

If your tracking shows "delivered" but you have not received the parcel, please wait 24 hours — carriers occasionally mark parcels delivered early. If it still has not arrived after 24 hours, contact us and we will open a trace with the carrier.

Orders typically ship within 1 business day of being placed. If your order has not shipped within 3 business days, contact support and we will investigate.

## Cancel or Modify an Order

Orders can be cancelled or modified within 1 hour of being placed, before they enter fulfilment. Log in to your account, go to Orders, and select "Cancel Order" or "Edit Order". After 1 hour the order is locked for picking and we cannot make changes.

If your order has already shipped, you can return it once it arrives using our standard return process. If you need to change the delivery address after shipping has started, contact us as soon as possible — we may be able to redirect the parcel depending on the carrier.

## Password Reset

Go to acme.example/login and click "Forgot password". Enter your registered email address and we will send a password reset link within 5 minutes. The link expires in 24 hours — if it expires, simply request a new one.

If you do not receive the email, check your spam or junk folder. If it is not there, make sure you are entering the email address registered to your account. If you are still locked out, contact support with your name and order history and we can verify your identity and update your email address.

## Billing & Payment

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, and PayPal. Apple Pay and Google Pay are available on supported browsers and devices.

To manage your payment methods, log in and go to Account > Payment Methods. You can add, remove, or set a default card. Changes take effect immediately for your next purchase. Saved cards are stored securely — Acme Store never sees or stores your full card number.

If a charge appears on your statement that you do not recognise, check your order history first as the charge may appear under "ACME STORE" or "ACME.EXAMPLE". If you still cannot identify it, contact us with the date and amount and we will investigate.

## Discount Codes & Promotions

To apply a discount code, add items to your cart and enter the code in the "Promo code" field on the checkout page before completing payment. The discount is applied immediately. Only one promo code can be used per order. Codes cannot be applied retroactively to completed orders.

If your code is not working, check that it has not expired, that the items in your cart are eligible (some codes exclude sale items or specific categories), and that the minimum order value is met. If the code still does not work, contact support with the code and we will check it for you.

Sale prices and bundle discounts are applied automatically at checkout — no code required.

## Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrived damaged or is not working as described, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. Attach a photo of the damage or defect and your order number. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund — your choice. You do not need to return the damaged item in most cases.

If a product develops a fault after normal use, please check the manufacturer's warranty. Many products sold at Acme Store carry a 12-month manufacturer warranty. Contact us and we will help you with a warranty claim.

## Gift Cards

Acme Store gift cards are available in denominations of $25, $50, $100, and $200. They can be purchased online at acme.example/gift-cards or in any Acme Store location. Gift cards are delivered by email within 15 minutes of purchase.

To redeem a gift card, enter the code at checkout in the "Gift card or promo code" field. Gift card balances can be checked at acme.example/gift-cards. Gift cards do not expire and cannot be exchanged for cash.

## Loyalty Rewards

Acme Rewards members earn 1 point for every $1 spent. Points can be redeemed for store credit at a rate of 100 points = $1. There is no minimum redemption amount.

To check your points balance, log in and go to Account > Rewards. Points are added to your account within 24 hours of a completed delivery. Points earned on returned items are deducted when the return is processed. Sign up for free at acme.example/rewards.

## International Orders

We ship to Canada, the United Kingdom, Australia, and the European Union. International shipping rates and estimated delivery times are shown at checkout. Import duties and local taxes are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in the order total.

We do not currently ship to all countries. If your country is not available at checkout, it means we do not yet support shipping there. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when we expand to new regions.

## Account Registration

Creating an account lets you track orders, save addresses and payment methods, earn Rewards points, and access your order history. To register, go to acme.example/register and enter your name, email address, and a password.

You can also check out as a guest without creating an account. Guest orders can be tracked using the link in your confirmation email, but cannot be linked to a Rewards account after the fact.

If you want to delete your account and all associated data, contact support and we will process the request within 7 business days in accordance with our privacy policy.

## Contact & Support Hours

Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9 am – 6 pm Eastern Time. We are closed on US public holidays. You can reach us via this chat, by email at support@acme.example, or by calling 1-800-ACME-HELP.

We aim to respond to email enquiries within 1 business day. Live chat response times are typically under 5 minutes during business hours.
