# Commercial licensing

BotWire is open source under the [GNU Affero General Public License v3](LICENSE) (AGPL-3.0). A commercial license is available when AGPL terms do not fit your use case.

## What AGPL means for commercial users

Under the AGPL, if you modify BotWire and make it available to users over a network (for example, as part of a hosted customer-support service), you must provide the corresponding source code to those users. Using BotWire in a closed-source product, or combining it with proprietary code in ways that trigger AGPL copyleft, generally requires either complying with AGPL obligations or obtaining a separate commercial license.

AGPL is a good fit when you can open-source your changes or when your use does not trigger network-copyleft requirements. It is often not a good fit when you need to distribute a proprietary application that embeds or depends on BotWire without source disclosure.

## What a commercial license provides

A commercial license from Object IT Limited typically allows:

- Use of BotWire in **proprietary** products and services
- Distribution **without** AGPL source-code disclosure obligations for your application
- A **direct licensing relationship** with the copyright holder, separate from the public AGPL terms

Specific terms (scope, pricing, support, updates) are agreed per customer. This document is informational only and does not constitute an offer.
